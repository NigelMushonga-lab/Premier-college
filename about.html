Our Identity

Premier College is Zimbabwe’s most elite private tutoring institution, committed to delivering excellence, purpose-driven learning, and academic mastery. Our model merges high-performance teaching with emotional intelligence, spiritual grounding, and critical thinking.

Mission

To inspire and empower students to impact the world through knowledge, character, and vision.

Vision

To provide a safe, supportive learning community that empowers students to become problem-solvers, engaged citizens, and compassionate people who own their future.

Motto

“Seeking the extraordinary in the ordinary.”
