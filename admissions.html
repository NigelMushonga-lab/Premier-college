Digital Campus Application

  1. Fill in and submit the online application form.
  2. Submit:

  4. Collect your Handbook and First Term invoice from our office.

Lower 6 Admissions

  1. Download and complete the application form and brochure.
  2. Submit required documents including O-level results and personal details.
  3. Attend an interview with the Director of Studies.
  4. Select subject combinations and finalize tuition payment.
