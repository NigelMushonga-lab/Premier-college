Our Academic Programmes

At Premier College, we offer a comprehensive academic program that supports both traditional and digital learning across various levels. Our curriculum is designed to challenge students while developing critical thinking, creativity, and discipline.

We Offer:

  • Grade 6 – Form 6 Tutoring (ZIMSEC & Cambridge)
  • IGCSE, AS & A Level Coaching
  • Individualized Learning Plans
  • Homeschooling Support Programmes
  • SAT, IELTS & University Prep

Key Features:

  • Small Class Sizes
  • Flexible Timetables
  • Online and Physical Classes
  • Regular Assessments and Feedback
  • Supportive and Passionate Educators

All programmes are tailored to meet each student’s academic goals while fostering confidence and independence.
